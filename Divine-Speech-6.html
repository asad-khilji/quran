Transitions in the Quran

Adal and Kist in Surah Al Hujarat

  • Two groups of Muslims are fighting
  • A and B are fighting then C should make peace
  • If A fights B again then B & C should fight A
  • Then when A stops fighting and wants to make peace again
  • Then C should make peace between A and B with justice and make sure to not be unfair
  • Making peace occured twice in the ayah
  • The second time Allah added to make peace with justice and not to be unfair
  • First C was neutral but second time C was involved in the fight too
  • So Allah highlighted and reminded to make peace with justice and not to be unfair
  • Because the second time around C had a tendency of being unfair
  • Adl means open and public justice
  • Kist means public or private justice
  • The court is called adala means it is open and public
  • In the first case Allah said do kist but in second case Allah said do adal
